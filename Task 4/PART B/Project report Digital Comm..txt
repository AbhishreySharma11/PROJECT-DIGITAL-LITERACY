Project Report: Digital Communication Reflection - 


In your project report, you can include this reflection (approx. 130 words):

A common scenario where poor digital communication causes significant issues involves "Reply-All" email chains in a professional or academic setting. In a hypothetical situation, a student might accidentally send a frustrated or unprofessional comment about an assignment to their entire batch and the professor, rather than just a close friend, by hitting "Reply-All." This breach of etiquette can lead to immediate disciplinary action, a strained relationship with faculty, and a damaged reputation among peers.

To prevent this, the student should have followed the "Three-Second Rule": always double-check the recipient list before clicking send. Furthermore, sensitive or emotional topics should never be handled via email while one is angry. Communicating through a private, offline channel or waiting twenty-four hours to draft a professional inquiry would have resolved the issue without causing a public conflict.
