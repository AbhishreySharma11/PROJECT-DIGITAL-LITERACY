Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist - 

Do's - 
	
1. Do use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.
	
2. Do think twice before posting; remember that your "digital footprint" is permanent.

3. Do verify the authenticity of a news story or viral post before sharing it with others.
	
4. Do use social media to network professionally by following industry leaders and alumni.
	
5. Do keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your latest skills and academic projects.
	
Don'ts - 

1. Don't post confidential information like your home address, phone number, or student ID.

2. Don't engage in "cyber-arguments" or use aggressive language in public comment sections.

3. Don't post photos or videos of others without their explicit permission.

4. Don't complain about professors, internships, or future employers on public platforms.

5. Don't use unprofessional or offensive handles/usernames for accounts linked to your real name.
